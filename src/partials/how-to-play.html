How to play

Match 3 or more cookies to clear them from the board.

Utilize unique power-ups to solve challenging puzzles.

Meet specific level goals to progress through the game.

Engage in special event levels for extra rewards.

Playing games should always be a fun and enjoyable experience. However, it's important to play responsibly and in moderation. Remember to take regular breaks, be mindful of your time, and avoid letting gaming interfere with your daily responsibilities or well-being. If you find that gaming is negatively affecting your life, consider seeking guidance or support. Play wisely, stay balanced, and enjoy the game responsibly!
