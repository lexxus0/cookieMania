About This Game

Cookie Mania is a delightful match-3 game where players match cookies to serve customers in a bustling bakery setting. With hundreds of levels, each offering unique challenges and objectives, players can enjoy a deliciously sweet gameplay experience.

How to play

Match 3 or more cookies to clear them from the board.

Utilize unique power-ups to solve challenging puzzles.

Meet specific level goals to progress through the game.

Engage in special event levels for extra rewards.
